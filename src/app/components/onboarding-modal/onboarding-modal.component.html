How Do I Use This?

1. Create

Have your video collections provisioned to the database. Select a reference video clip and a collection of videos to search for similarities.

1. Train

The algorithm will generate samples for you to grade until you think the samples reflect the action of interest to you.

3. Export

Get a report of all matching clips in your collection, using the algorithm trained on the samples you graded.

What is a reference video clip?   

What Is A Reference Video Clip?

You will select a time in a video that shows the type of action of interest to you. The program will use the 10 seconds around the time you choose as the reference clip. This reference clip will be compared with other clips in your video collection.
